WHO WE ARE

Built by Students. Driven by Purpose.

Konsortium exists to be the backbone of NGOs that are changing the world — we handle the heavy lifting so they can focus on what matters most.

Our Mission

We believe every NGO, no matter how small, deserves world-class support. Konsortium provides the infrastructure — marketing, operations, finance management, and strategic guidance — that allows charitable organizations to maximize their impact without drowning in logistics.

Student-Led

Founded and run by teenagers who refuse to wait for change. We're proof that age is just a number when it comes to making a difference.

Comprehensive Support

From marketing strategies to financial planning, operational frameworks to creative content — we cover every angle an NGO needs help with.

Giving Back

Beyond helping NGOs, we're launching our own charity initiatives — collecting donations from schools and giving back to the communities we serve.

WHAT WE DO

How We Help NGOs Thrive.

We provide end-to-end support so NGOs can focus on their mission while we handle the rest.

01

Marketing & Outreach

Social media strategy, brand identity, content creation, and digital marketing campaigns that amplify an NGO's voice and reach broader audiences.

02

Operations Management

We streamline workflows, set up efficient processes, and build operational frameworks that help NGOs run smoothly without burning out their teams.

03

Financial Planning

Budget management, fundraising strategy, financial reporting, and resource allocation guidance to keep NGOs financially healthy and transparent.

04

Strategic Guidance

Long-term planning, goal setting, partnership development, and growth strategies that help NGOs scale their impact sustainably.

05

Creative & Content

Video production, graphic design, website development, and storytelling that brings an NGO's mission to life through compelling visual content.

06

Tech & Digital

Website development, app solutions, digital infrastructure setup, and technology consulting to bring NGOs into the modern digital age.

OUR JOURNEY

How It All Started.

The Spark

An Idea Was Born

Two students realized that countless NGOs struggle not because of lack of passion, but lack of operational support. Konsortium was conceived as the solution.

Building the Foundation

Expanding Horizons

Ventures Launch

Konsortia Films hit YouTube. IRISE Capital began advisory operations. The ecosystem began to take shape.
